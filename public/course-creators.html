What You Can Track

Any habit. Any goal. Verified with real data.

HabitWager works for anything you want to be held accountable to. And we back it up with actual proof, not just your word.

🏋️

Hit the gym 4x a week

Stake money on your workout consistency. We verify using gym check-in records, Apple Watch or Whoop workout logs, or Garmin activity data.

🥗

Eat better, track it honestly

Commit to cleaner eating or hitting your macros. Verified via CGM data (Levels, Dexcom), food logs from MyFitnessPal, or daily photo check-ins of your meals.

📱

Cut your screen time

Set a daily screen time limit and prove you hit it. Verified via iPhone Screen Time screenshots or Android Digital Wellbeing reports sent in your daily check-in.

📸

Practice photography daily

Commit to shooting one photo a day to build your eye and your portfolio. Verified by sharing the day's shot in your coach check-in.

📝

Post 1x per day on social media

Build your personal brand by posting consistently on LinkedIn, X, TikTok, or Instagram. Verified via post link submitted in your daily check-in.

🧘

Meditate or journal every morning

Start each day right. Verified via Calm or Headspace session screenshots, or a brief journal excerpt shared with your coach.

How Verification Works

Every habit gets an audit method agreed on at setup. You send proof to your AI coach daily: a screenshot, a data export, a check-in note, or a photo. The coach reviews it and either verifies you (stake kept) or flags a miss (stake lost). No gaming the system. No honor system. Just real accountability.
