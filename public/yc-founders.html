Commit to cleaner eating or hitting your macros. Verified via CGM data (Levels, Dexcom), food logs from MyFitnessPal, or daily photo check-ins of your meals.

🧘

How Verification Works

Every habit gets an audit method agreed on at setup. You send proof to your AI coach daily: a screenshot, a data export, a check-in note, or a photo. The coach reviews it and either verifies you (stake kept) or flags a miss (stake lost). No gaming the system. No honor system. Just real accountability.
